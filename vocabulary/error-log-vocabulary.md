# 报错日志词汇库

> 本词汇库专注于 JavaWeb 开发中常见的报错日志关键词汇

---

## 📂 目录结构说明

本词汇库按照错误类型进行分类：

### 1. 堆栈追踪词汇 (Stack Trace Vocabulary)
关键词：`Caused by`, `Exception in thread`, `at`, `at line`

### 2. 编译时错误 (Compile-time Errors)
关键词：`Syntax error`, `Cannot find symbol`, `Incompatible types`

### 3. 运行时错误 (Runtime Errors)
关键词：`NullPointerException`, `ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException`, `ClassCastException`

### 4. 网络与 IO 错误 (Network & IO Errors)
关键词：`Timeout`, `Connection refused`, `Stream closed`, `SocketException`

### 5. 数据库相关错误 (Database Errors)
关键词：`Syntax error in SQL`, `Constraint violation`, `Deadlock`, `Connection pool`

### 6. Spring/框架相关错误 (Framework Errors)
关键词：`Bean creation`, `Dependency injection`, `Auto-configuration`
